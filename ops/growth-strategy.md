## North Star

Build KSPF into a **recognised, sought-after solo creative studio** in Melbourne — known for brand strategy, identity, and UX work for founders and small brands in fashion, beauty, and lifestyle. The goal is a sustainable pipeline of $3k–$15k projects, earning enough to fund personal growth and creative work (music production, North Star) without burning out.

**12-month target:** 8–10 client projects delivered. Steady inbound pipeline. kspf.au live with full case study section. KSPF name circulating in the Melbourne creative/brand scene.

**24-month target:** 2–3 returning/referral clients per quarter. Studio positioned as the go-to for indie brand founders wanting strategic craft. Considering selective scale (associate collaborators or retainer work).

## Positioning

**Who KSPF serves:**
Solo founders and small creative brands — especially in fashion, beauty, and lifestyle — who care deeply about craft and visual culture. They need **strategy + execution**, not just a production service. Melbourne-based preferred but not required.

**Why KSPF:**
- Deep UX background = brand strategy grounded in how people actually experience things
- Rare combination of strategy, identity, web, and packaging in one person
- Thoughtful, unhurried — "whisper don't shout" approach mirrors the clients we attract

**Positioning statement (working):**
> *KSPF is a Melbourne creative studio for founders who want their brand built right. Strategy-first, craft-led.*

## Three Growth Pillars

### 1. Portfolio & Proof of Work
*Without visible work, outreach doesn't land.*

- Publish Alt Cosmetics case study (when delivered)
- Add UX case studies to kspf.au (AP-REPS, Bally Cara)
- Document work-in-progress as you go — don't wait for the project to close
- Pinterest as a low-effort discovery channel for visual work

### 2. Systematic Lead Generation
*A pipeline only works if it runs consistently.*

- Build and maintain target outreach list (10–15 names per cycle)
- Direct outreach: 5 contacts/week from April
- Automate where possible (website auditor, email drafts) but keep personalisation high
- Nurture referral sources: Anh's network, past clients (AP-REPS, Bally Cara)

### 3. Revenue Diversification
*Don't rely on project work alone while pipeline is early.*

- **Askable** — activate as consistent income stream while client pipeline builds
- **Fuel gigs** — quick cash work as needed
- **Retainer** — pursue at least one Studio Partnership client by Q3
- **Holmesglen support** — make use of David Carnegie for financial planning + accountability
