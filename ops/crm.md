## Status Rules

- `Waiting on reply`: relationship exists, but the next move depends on an inbound email or response.
- `Ready to send`: contact exists and the lead is usable now after light edits.
- `Ready to personalize`: draft exists, but it still sounds too generic and needs one sharper observation.
- `Research contact`: likely worth pursuing, but a direct email or named contact is missing.
- `Needs audit`: lead exists in the seed list, but the audit output is missing or stale.
- `Hold for later wave`: keep the lead, but do not spend time on it this week.
- `Referral source to nurture`: not a cold outreach target; keep warm and ask for introductions at the right time.
