## Packages

### Starter — Brand Audit
- Audit of existing brand identity, website, and competitor landscape
- Written recommendations doc (10–15 pages)
- 1x 60-min strategy call
- **Deliverable:** Recommendations PDF + call recording

### Core — Brand Identity
- Discovery + brand strategy brief
- Logo system (primary, secondary, sub-mark)
- Colour + typography
- 1-page brand guidelines
- **Deliverable:** Figma file + exported asset pack

### Core — Web Build
- Up to 6 pages, custom coded or Squarespace/Shopify
- Mobile responsive, accessibility reviewed
- 1 round of revisions
- **Deliverable:** Live site + handover doc

### Full — Strategy + Identity + Web
- Everything in Core Identity + Core Web
- Brand strategy document
- Packaging design direction (if applicable)
- **Deliverable:** All of the above + 2x strategy calls

### Retainer
- 4 hours per month of ongoing support
- Covers: design iterations, strategy check-ins, small builds, content
- Rolling monthly, cancel anytime with 30 days notice

## Proposal Template

> See `projects/` for past project files (Alt Cosmetics, AP-REPS) as reference examples.

A proposal should include:
1. **Context** — what we heard from the brief / discovery call
2. **Scope** — what's included (and what's not)
3. **Deliverables** — what they'll receive and in what format
4. **Timeline** — key milestones and delivery date
5. **Investment** — price, payment terms (50% upfront, 50% on delivery)
6. **Next steps** — how to proceed / sign off

---

## Payment Terms

- 50% deposit to begin
- 50% on delivery
- Retainers billed monthly in advance
- Late payment: 10% added after 14 days overdue

---

## Notes

- Always include a retainer option in every proposal — even one retainer changes monthly cashflow
- Scope creep clause: any work beyond agreed scope is billed at $150/hr
- For new clients, require a signed brief before starting any design work
