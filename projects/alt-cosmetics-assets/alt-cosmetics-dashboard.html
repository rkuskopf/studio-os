Mark Directions (Current) Ready for mockups
Direction A

Ancient / Carved / Archaeological

Raw, primitive, worn-into-stone quality. Egyptian scarab seals, ancient intaglio gems. Incised, not raised.

Direction B

Victorian / Script / Medieval

Fine line heritage script. Ornate calligraphic letterforms, swash construction. Mauclot monogram sheet reference.

Direction C

Architectural / Art Deco / SRA SRA

Formal oval cartouche with structured frame. Letters at compass points. Geometric, authoritative.

Anh's feedback: Wants it abstract and flowing — "like the Loewe where you can't really tell what it says." Not overly structured or masculine. Sweet spot: structured container (oval/circle) with flowing letterforms inside.
