### Research Answers (resolved Fri 13 Mar)

**Q: "What's this called? window grill?"**
The style in the slide is **wrought iron scrollwork** — specifically built from **S-scrolls** and **C-scrolls**. The collective design term is *scrollwork* or *ornamental ironwork*. Historical lineage: Art Nouveau / *ferronnerie d'art* (French decorative ironwork). Distinct from Art Deco (geometric) — the organic curved form with foliage is more Art Nouveau.

**Task: Find the Monastery brand reference**
Brand is **Monastery** (spelled without the "e" — [monasterymade.com](https://monasterymade.com)). SF-based luxury botanical skincare by esthetician Athena Hewett. Known for embossed/stamped heraldic crest mark on glass bottles — "old-world apothecary meets modern minimalism."
- Reference imagery: [Dieline](https://thedieline.com/monasterys-luxe-packaging-visually-highlights-the-purity-of-the-ingredients-within/) · [Coveteur](https://coveteur.com/monastery-skincare-product-reviews) · AU stockist [Lume Sydney](https://lumesydney.com.au/collections/monastery-skincare)
