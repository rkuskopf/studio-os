# UX Portfolio — Case Study Compilation

## Goal
Consolidate all UX case study drafts, research artifacts, and portfolio drafts into one structured source set. Analyse all versions and rewrite the strongest narrative into a presentation-ready research portfolio.

## Reference / North Star

Marcus Procida – Design Research Portfolio
https://drive.google.com/file/d/192IICxHDi9NolzCbIsuq3wV94T3gbmnc/view?usp=drive_web

Attachments: MProcida_CV:Design Folio_2025.pdf, MProcida_CV_Short Folio_2025.pdf

Use as reference for: narrative structure, slide pacing, storytelling flow, research explanation clarity, insight framing, case study layout.

## Claude Instruction
Review all case study sources below. Identify overlapping versions and strongest narrative material. Rewrite into a presentation-ready case study structure similar in clarity and storytelling to the Marcus Procida research portfolio. Focus on problem framing, research approach, insights, design decisions, and outcomes. Do not invent work. Flag missing evidence.
