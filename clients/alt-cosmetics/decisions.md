### Naming
| Decision | Detail |
|----------|--------|

| Connotations | Alternative, subversive, different, alt = keyboard |

### Positioning (Perceptual Map)
| Axis | ALT. Target |
|------|-------------|
| Ritual/Lifestyle ←→ Clinical/Efficacy | Center-right (slightly clinical) |
| Bold/Editorial ←→ Quiet/Restrained | Upper (editorial, bold) |
| Reference brands | Acne Studios, Nolew, Prada Beauty |
| NOT like | Aesop (too ritual), GrandeLash (too mass/functional) |

### Visual Direction (Anh's original moodboard)
- Chrome/silver metallic packaging
- Minimal, clinical aesthetic
- Font noted: Eina 01, Semi Bold, Outline (later evolved to Aktiv Grotesk Extended)
- Text colour: White/Grey
- Tube sizes: 3ml/4ml/5ml
- Lash serum as hero product

### Brand Personality
| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| 3 words customer should feel | Luxury/premium, Special, ⚠️ **INCOMPLETE — needs follow-up** |
| Note | Emotional experience of holding/opening/using — not efficacy |

### Personality Sliders
| Spectrum | Position |
|----------|----------|
| Elite ←→ Mass Appeal | Slightly Elite |
| Serious ←→ Playful | Center (balanced) |
| Conventional ←→ Rebel | Slightly Rebel |
| Friend ←→ Authority | Slightly Authority |
| Mature & Classic ←→ Young & Innovative | Center-right (leaning innovative) |

### Mission
> Cater to different concerns for individuals throughout different stages of their life. Deliver niche, fill gaps in the market, tailored to specific real life needs.

⚠️ Note: Check if this is reflected in creative brief

### Why (Brand Purpose)
- Clients bought other serums, want something that doesn't irritate
- Buy from someone they know and trust
- Trust in advice and choosing products
- Work with salons as a way to develop trust with people

### How (Product Approach)
- Vegan, peptides, no harsh chemicals, plant botanicals
- Standard applicator
- Personal experience and knowledge — trying products, seeing reactions
- Lots of testing, forming habits with people
- More specifically tailored to lashes (vs generic serums)

### Mark System
| Decision | Two-element identity |
|----------|---------------------|
| Primary | Hard geometric wordmark |
| Secondary | Ornamental mark (discovery element) |
| Relationship | Should NOT look like they came from the same place — tension is intentional |
| Scale | Must hold at small scale, debossed/hot-stamped |
| Status | ✅ Confirmed |

### Mark Direction — Client Preferences
| Liked | SRA SRA, Loewe, JB monogram |
|-------|----------------------------|
| Feedback | "Abstract and flowing, like the Loewe where you can't really tell what it says" |
| Avoid | Overly structured or masculine; anything too old/material-based (no ancient textures, no primitive marks) |
| Sweet spot | Simple, hand-drawn monogram vibes — abstract and flowing but refined |
| Contrast | A little contrast between mark and wordmark is interesting |
| Status | ✅ Confirmed from concept presentation |

### Brand Positioning
| Decision | Design-forward premium beauty |
|----------|------------------------------|
| Between | Clinical (Clinique) ←→ Lifestyle (Grande) |
| Character | Earns place through restraint, finish, intention |
| Avoid | Not Aesop, The Ordinary, or Grande territory |
| Status | ✅ Confirmed |

### Tone of Voice
| Decision | Precise, assured, uncluttered |
|----------|------------------------------|
| Style | States not sells. No exclamation marks. |
| Avoid | Wellness clichés ("ritual," "glow," "nourishing") |
| Warmth | Like a trusted expert — not effusive |
| Status | ✅ Confirmed |

### Colour
| Decision | Monochrome / near-neutral, low contrast |
|----------|----------------------------------------|
| Direction | Some warmth. No brights. |
| Likely | Black, white, silver — bright accents possible |
| Unlikely | Earth tones |
| Status | ⚠️ Open — no exploration done yet |

### Sustainability

Three canonical directions in active development:
