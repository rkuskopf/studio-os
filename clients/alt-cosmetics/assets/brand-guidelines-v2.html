ALT. Cosmetics 02
01 — Brand Overview

About ALT.

Design-forward premium beauty. Earns its place through restraint, finish, and intention.

ALT. Cosmetics launches with a single hero product: a plant-based, peptide-rich lash serum. Founded by Anh Tran — experienced lash technician and cosmetic tattoo artist.

Positioning
Between clinical and lifestyle — neither pharmacy-cold nor mass-market loud
Character
Quietly confident. Doesn't explain itself. Thrives in simplicity.
Long-term
Mecca retail placement. Brand built for eventual sale.

ALT. Cosmetics 04
02 — Logo

Mark System

Two-element identity: hard geometric wordmark + ornamental secondary mark.

These two elements should not look like they came from the same place — the tension is intentional. Inspired by atelier and silversmith traditions.

Application
Hot-stamping, debossing, or engraving onto chrome surfaces

03 — Typography

Primary Typeface

Aktiv Grotesk Extended

Aktiv Grotesk Extended is the primary typeface for all brand communications. All-caps treatment throughout. Medium-bold weight for headlines.

The extended width provides presence at small scale. Geometric construction is the character — not applied decoration.

Source
Adobe Fonts or Dalton Maag

ALT. Cosmetics 09
05 — Tone of Voice

Voice Principles

Product copy
"Peptide-rich formula. Visible results in four to six weeks."
Instruction
"Apply once daily to clean, dry lashes. One stroke along the lash line."
Character
Precise, assured, uncluttered. States rather than suggests.
Flexibility
Can be cheeky, flirty — adapts to seasons and moments. IFYKYK energy.
Avoid
Wellness clichés: ritual, glow, nourishing, clean beauty
