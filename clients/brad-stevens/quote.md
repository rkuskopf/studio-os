## CMS Options (for Project 2)

| CMS | Fit | Notes |
|-----|-----|-------|
| **Sanity** | Strong | Flexible schema, great DX, free tier generous. Good for image-heavy portfolios. |
| **Keystatic** | Good | Git-based, markdown-native, simpler for smaller sites. No database needed. |
| **Contentful** | Okay | Well-known, solid, but overkill for a portfolio. Paid tiers add up. |
| **Payload** | Consider | If they want self-hosted + full control. More dev time. |

Recommend **Sanity** unless Brad's team has a preference or existing tooling.
