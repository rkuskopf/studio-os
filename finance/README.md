# Finance

Monthly tracker for KSPF financials. Feeds directly into David Carnegie check-in sessions.

David asks for 6 metrics every session — keep them updated here.

---

## Metrics David Asks For

| Metric | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| Gross cash in | Total revenue received this month |
| Debtors | Money owed to KSPF (invoiced but not yet paid) |
| Cash out | Total expenses paid this month |
| Creditors | Money KSPF owes to others |
| Drawings | Amount Rowan took out of the business for personal use |
| Cost of goods sold (COGS) | Direct costs tied to delivering work (e.g. printing, software, freelancers) |
