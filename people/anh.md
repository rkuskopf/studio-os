# Anh

**Role:** Client  
**Project:** Branding: Alt Cosmetics (Asana project ID: 1213237592487953)

## About
- Salon owner (existing business)
- Studied fashion design at uni — has a good design eye
- Perfectionist, high attention to quality
- Wants to launch a separate cosmetics product (not tied to her personal brand)

## The Product
- A single cosmetics product, 4ml, $150
- Chrome packaging, possibly with clear suction wrap
- "Soft launch" — going to suppliers and selling through her salon first
- **Long-term goal: Get into Mecca**

## Brand Direction
- Working name: **Alt Cosmetics** (open to rename based on Rowan's input)
- Original concept logo: medium-weight sans serif "ALT. cosmetics" — Anh and Rowan agreed it felt overdone
- Positioning: **"whisper don't shout" / ifykyk** — brand doesn't over-explain
- Reference: [Fluff Cosmetics](https://www.itsallfluff.com/) — likes the product/personality, not the website
- Competitors: she thinks they're all ugly
- Tactile quality important — wants people to want to hold and look at it
- **Deadline: ~20 March 2026**

## Scope (this engagement)
- Brand identity + packaging
- NOT website (yet — she wants a quote but it's out of scope for now)
- Future: Shopify website, separate from main business
