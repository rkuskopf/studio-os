## Project Overview

Standalone beauty brand launching with a single hero product: a plant-based, peptide-rich lash serum. Founded by Anh Tran — experienced lash technician and cosmetic tattoo artist. Brand is completely separate from her existing service business, Anh's Artistry.

**Long-term goal:** Mecca retail placement (~3 years). Eventual brand sale.

**Positioning:** Design-forward premium beauty. Sits between overtly clinical (Clinique, pharmacy-adjacent) and mass-lifestyle (Grande, Zali). Neither shouts nor explains itself. Earns its place through restraint, finish, and intention.

**Brand in one line:** Beautiful because it works. Designed because it deserves to be.

## Creative Direction

**Personality axes (from strategy session):**
- Bold ←→ **Restrained**
- Clinical ←→ **Middle** (genuine efficacy, not pharmacy)
- Mass ←→ **Elite**
- Conversational ←→ **Authoritative**

**Brand character:** Quietly confident. Doesn't explain itself. Thrives in simplicity. Not afraid of appearing shallow. Doesn't follow trends, doesn't deliberately subvert them either. Aesthetics as an extension of sense and pleasure.

**Visual references:** Alaïa, Prada, retaW — high-contrast, editorial, considered.

**Typography:**
- Primary: Structured sans-serif, medium-to-bold weight, all-caps lockups preferred, deliberate tracking
- Secondary: Mono or typewriter-adjacent for functional text (ingredients, regulatory)
- Current candidates: Aktiv Grotesk Extended, Hiragino Kaku Gothic ProN

**Colour:** Restrained, material-adjacent. Near-neutrals with considered accent. No brights. Not pure black/white — some warmth or material quality should be present. Direction: monochrome, low contrast.

**Finish & Materiality — UNRESOLVED:**
- Brief specifies matte/satin, hot-stamping or debossing
- Anh has been considering reflective chrome options
- This is a fundamental contradiction requiring client resolution before artwork can proceed
- Chrome/reflective is not ruled out, but needs to be a deliberate decision, not a drift

**Tone of voice:** Precise, confident, uncluttered. States rather than suggests. No exclamation marks. No wellness clichés ("ritual," "glow," "nourishing"). Warm in the way a trusted expert is warm — not effusive. Specificity over vagueness.

## Strategic Tensions

- Clinical authority vs. editorial/alternative aesthetic
- Minimal vs. memorable
- Salon-trusted vs. Mecca-ready
- Industrial materiality vs. sustainability expectations (Mecca has requirements)
- Friend-client relationship vs. professional studio boundaries

---

## Competitive Landscape

- **Grande Cosmetics, Zali** — mass lifestyle, visually undifferentiated, over-promise
- **Clinique, pharmacy-adjacent** — clinical authority, cold/uninviting aesthetics
- **ALT. whitespace** — design-forward + genuine clinical credibility. Not currently well-occupied in Australian market.
- **Positioning note:** "Prostaglandin-free" and "clean" are table stakes now — not a differentiator
